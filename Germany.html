Neuschwanstein Castle

Description:
Neuschwanstein Castle is a 19th-century historicist palace on a rugged hill of the foothills of the Alps in the very south of Germany.

7 Days Suggested Itinerary in France

Day 1: Arrival in Berlin

Morning: Arrive in Berlin. Check into your accommodation.

Afternoon: Visit the iconic Brandenburg Gate, and explore the nearbyReichstag Building, Stroll along the Unter den Linden boulevard.

Evening: Have dinner at a traditional German restaurant and enjoy Berlin's vibrant nightlife in the Kreuzberg district.

Day 2: Explore Berlin

Morning: Visit the Berlin Wall Memorial and the East Side Gallery for a historical and artistic experience.

Afternoon: Explore Museum Island, home to the Pergamon Museum and the Altes Museum .

Evening: Dine in the hip Prenzlauer Berg area, known for its trendy eateries and relaxed vibe.

Day 3: Dresden

Morning: Travel to Dresden (2 hours by train) and check into your hotel.

Afternoon: Visit the stunning Zwinger Palace, Semper Opera House, and the Frauenkirche. Take a walk along the Elbe River.

Evening: Enjoy Saxon specialties like Dresdner Stollen or Sauerbraten at a local restaurant.

Day 4: Nuremberg

Morning: Take a train to Nuremberg (3 hours). Explore the medieval Nuremberg Castle and walk along the Old Town.

Afternoon: Visit the Documentation Center Nazi Party Rally Grounds for a deep dive into Germany's WWII history.

Evening: Try Nuremberg bratwurst with sauerkraut and a glass of local beer.


Day 5: Munich

Morning: Travel to Munich (2 hours by train). Check into your hotel and start with a visit to the Marienplatz and the Glockenspiel.

Afternoon:Explore the English Garden, one of the largest urban parks in the world, and enjoy a stein of beer at the Chinese Tower beer garden.

Evening: Dine at a traditional Bavarian beer hall like Hofbräuhaus, where you can enjoy pretzels, sausages, and schnitzel.

Day 6: Neuschwanstein Castle & Bavarian Alps

Morning: Take a day trip to the Neuschwanstein Castle (2 hours by train/bus). Explore this fairy-tale castle and enjoy the breathtaking views of the Bavarian Alps.

Afternoon: Visit the nearby Hohenschwangau Castle or hike to Marienbrücke for the best views of Neuschwanstein.

Evening:Return to Munich and enjoy a relaxing dinner at a cozy Bavarian restaurant.


Day 7: Departure

Morning: Depending on your flight time, explore Nymphenburg Palace or visit the BMW Museum in Munich.

Afternoon: Enjoy some last-minute shopping or a leisurely stroll through Viktualienmarkt before heading to the airport.

Tips:

  • Transport: Germany's train system is efficient. Consider a Eurail Germany Pass or book high-speed ICE trains in advance for cheaper fares.
  • Food to try: Weißwurst (white sausage), spätzle, black forest cake, and strudel.
  • Best time to visit: Late spring (May-June) or early fall (September-October) for pleasant weather and festivals like Oktoberfest.
